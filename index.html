A community workshop in Haworth for sharing skills, working on projects, and making new friends over a cup of tea.

Join Our Shed

Our Story

Haworth Men's Shed provides a safe and friendly environment where men can socialize and work on meaningful projects at their own pace. We focus on wellbeing, community support, and the joy of making things by hand.

What's On at the Shed

We provide the tools and space; you provide the creativity. There is something for everyone, regardless of skill level.

Hands-on

Woodworking & Repair

Our workshop is equipped for everything from birdhouse building to restoring old furniture. Bring your own project or help with community builds.

Creative

Metalwork & Fixing

From basic welding and metal art to electronics repair. We share knowledge to help fix those items you thought were beyond saving.

Outdoors

Community Gardening

A great way to get some fresh air while growing vegetables and flowers. Help us keep the local community blooming.

Social

The "Brew & Chat"

The most important part of the Shed. Sometimes you don't need a tool; you just need a cup of tea and a proper conversation.
